Master's Thesis


Programming Language
Python
Info
During my curricular internship I worked on a recommender system with Fidelity Salus S.R.L., an italian company that provides Fidelity Card and advertisement services to pharmacies all over the italian soil.
The recommender system, written in Python3 and using the Pandas library for data manipulation, makes use of model-based collaborative filtering to return a custom ranking of the N best items to recommend as a targeted special offer to a particular fidelity card user. In other words, the recommender system returns the products that the client is most likely to buy if properly advertised. For the model training we used the Bayesian Personalized Ranking (BPR) algorithm.

In my work for the Master's Degree I investigated the effect of data clustering on the training of the aforementioned model. We analyzed the results of using density-based (DBSCAN), partitional (K-Means) and Hierarchical (Agglomerative) algorithms and analyzed pros and cons of each one of them in our particular study case.
